## üìà Key Insights & Findings
- Attrition is concentrated in specific **departments** and **job roles**.  
- Employees with **<1 year tenure** show highest churn.  
- **Salary bands** show strong correlation with attrition.  
- Certain **managers** have significantly higher team attrition.  
- Younger age groups (20‚Äì30) display more rapid turnover.  
- Some hiring cohorts experience low retention within the first year.  
- Compensation, job stress, and management quality are major drivers.
